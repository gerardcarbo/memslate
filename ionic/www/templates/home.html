Memslate > Memorize your Translations

Memslate can help you to translate words and phrases and, memorize them. How does it works?

Translate

Memslate is able to translate form and to multiple languages. Translations include multiple exceptions and fonetic trascription.

Memorize

Memslate keeps track of your translations. You can order, filter and review them whenever you want in order to memorize them.

And Play!

And finally Memslate can perform tests, based on your previous translations. The tests are performed in order to help you memorize them. Learn while playing!
